Rebecca Shen is an aspiring landscape architect whose broad interests lie in the synthesis of landscape and culture in the era of rapid social, ecological, and political shifts due to climate change. Specifically, she is interested in landscapes that foster interspecies (particularly with nonhuman animals) living communities, sustainable food production, and climate change mitigation at the community scale. Rebecca is pursuing a Master in Landscape Architecture at Harvard Graduate School of Design, graduating in 2023. She graduated from Washington University in St. Louis in 2020, earning a Bachelor of Science in Architecture Magna cum laude with minors in Computer Science and Landscape Architecture. During her free time, you will probably find Rebecca painting figures and places with watercolors or oils, attempting new jump rope tricks, delving into animal studies literature, or hiking the beautiful surroundings of her home in Upstate New York.

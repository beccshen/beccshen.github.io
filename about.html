Rebecca Shen is an aspiring landscape architect. Her broad interests lie in the synthesis of landscape and culture during this era of rapid social and ecological transformations under climate change. She is particularly interested in landscapes and systems of multispecies interactions, food production, and community-based environmental stewardship. Rebecca is pursuing a Master in Landscape Architecture at Harvard Graduate School of Design, graduating in 2023. She graduated from Washington University in St. Louis in 2020, earning a Bachelor of Science in Architecture Magna cum laude with minors in Computer Science and Landscape Architecture. During her free time, you will probably find Rebecca painting figures and places with watercolors or oils, delving into animal studies literature, or hiking her surroundings.

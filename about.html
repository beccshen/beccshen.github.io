My name is Rebecca Shen, an aspiring landscape architect whose broad interests lie in the synthetic relationships of landscapes in the era climate change and technological innovation. I am navigating my challenging role as a designer in these contexts, especially taking concern in how we influence the existences and livelihoods of our nonhuman co-inhabitants.

Currently, I am a landscape designer at Terrain Work, a landscape architecture, urban design, and public art studio in New York City. I am pursuing a Master in Landscape Architecture at Harvard Graduate School of Design, where I will be resuming my studies in the Spring of 2022. I graduated from Washington University in St. Louis in 2020, earning a Bachelor of Science in Architecture with minors in Computer Science and Landscape Architecture.
